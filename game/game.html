✨ SPECIAL POLLENS

RAINBOW POLLEN

6s of power mode. Birds flee from you. Touch them to eat for +10 pts each.

SPEED DEMON

Black pollen with yellow spikes and fire tail. Boosts your speed by 20% for 6s.

🔮

SPREAD POLLEN

Teal pollen with expanding ring. Doubles your collection radius for 6s — collect pollen from twice as far.

PROTECT POLLEN

Metallic silver pollen with vest plates. Birds cannot hurt you for 6s.

FREEZE POLLEN

Ice-blue crystal pollen. Slows all birds by 50% for 6s. Your speed is unaffected.

SHOCK WINGS

Electric charge pollen. Stuns and slows nearby birds. Wing arcs visible on bee for 6s.

Magenta spinning laser pollen. A rotating laser beam sweeps around the bee, destroying any bird it touches for 6s.

Groups of 2,3,5 or 7 small black birds with skinny tails fly in formation. Missile to the centre kills all. Hit a flank — only those birds die, rest continue in formation.
