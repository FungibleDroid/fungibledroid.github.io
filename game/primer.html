█ MAP THREATS
🐍 SNAKE PACK
4-segment chain chases bee relentlessly. Missile hits the head — it recoils, loses a segment, then resumes. Bomb blast removes 1 segment. Kill the leader to shorten the chain. Snake regrows segments over time. Gets stuck in U-shaped walls — it will retrace and find another route.
🤖 TBOT (POLLEN BOT)
Your helper drone follows inside your radar circle. Collects pollen for you — those pickups count as yours. Battery dies in 45s — touch it to revive. Appears as a blue dot on your radar. Stays within radar range; will retrace if it gets trapped.
🦜 WALL SENTRIES
Stationary turrets that perch on walls in groups of 3. Can't move but spit pink acid within 2 bee-widths. Kill on touch. A range ring pulses when you're in danger zone. Use missiles to clear them — they're worth the shot.
🔒 BIRD CAGES
Glass or circle enclosures trap 2–4 dormant birds. Birds sleep until a cage wall breaks. Break the cage → birds activate with random mutant powers and scatter. A wall bomb sits outside each cage — don't trigger it accidentally. Cages cluster near map centre where traffic is highest.
█ WALLS
GLASS — Breakable (1 hit). Bee bounces off. Use lines, crosses and L-shapes to channel bird paths. Relax mode has far more glass coverage. Glass never hardens.
SOLID — Unbreakable, amber glow. Permanent obstacles. No entity — bee, Tbot, bird or snake — can cross a solid wall. Use them to plan safe corridors.
█ EXPLOSIVES
  • 🧨 Dynamite — arm on touch, detonate by passing again. 150px blast. Snake loses 1 segment.
  • 💣 Wall Bomb — sits on wall, triggered by bee/missile/bird contact. 1.5s fuse. Breaks wall + chain-blasts nearby bombs.
  • ⚫ Big Bomb — rare Tbot reward. Shift+B / N to place. 4s fuse, 300px blast radius. Get clear!
█ MUTANT BIRDS — every 5th bird spawned is a mutant · power activates immediately · missile kill = +25 pts
🔥 FIRE
Breathes fire cone forward every 3.5s. Stay back or go sideways.
💜 LASER
Directional beam forward every 6s. Dodge sideways.
↩⚡ BACK-LASER
Fires beam BEHIND its flight path every 2s on/2s off. Watch your tail.
☠️ POISON
Toxic cloud spray every 5s. Contact with cloud kills.
💪 HEAVY
Larger body (r=22), slower. Hard to dodge — takes up more space.
⚡ SHOCK
Electric discharge every 2.5s within 180px. Kills instantly.
☢️ RADIATION
Emits expanding arc waves every 3s. Rotates 45° between bursts. 90° kill cone.
☣ AVIAN FLU
Zebra stripes + yellow bio-eye. Infects pollen. Eating infected = 10s death timer.
🔀 SPLITTER
Missile kill splits it into 2 smaller birds at ±50°. Kill early!
👻 STEALTH
Fades to 18% opacity after 3s. Reappears at 120px. Shows as ghost blip on radar.
💣 BOMBER
Grey eye + red dot. Drops mines every 6s. Mines pulse red, explode after 8s.
⛏ DIGGER
Circles a spot and digs a lethal hole (18px). Hole active for 18s. Avoid the arc.
😡 BERSERKER
Red eye grows brighter. Speeds up +12% per pollen eaten. No cap. Kill early!
🧲 MAGNET
Blue eye + ring. Pulls all nearby pollen (200px) to itself. Starves the bee.
💨 SMOKE
Grey body. Emits smoke puff every 0.55s when bee is nearby. Cloud obscures vision briefly.
❓ ERRATIC
Orange eye. Random ±60° jink every 1.5s and on wall bounces. Unpredictable flight.
NORMAL
Chases bee, eats pollen. White body, teal cap, orange beak. +15 pts missile kill.
█ SPECIAL POLLEN — spawn randomly · each lasts 6s · hover to collect
🌈 RAINBOW

⚡ SPEED
