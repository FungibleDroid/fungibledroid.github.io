BEE YARD DEFENSE CREW — FIELD OPERATIONS MANUAL

Objective
  • Fly your bee around the yard collecting pollen to score points.
  • Birds chase you and eat your pollen — kill them with missiles to loot what they carried.
  • Complete each wave's score target to advance to the next wave.
  • You have 3 lives. Lose all 3 → game over.
  • Pollen infected with ☣ starts a 10-second death timer — find the vaccine fast.
  • Your Tbot (robot helper) follows you, collects pollen, and counts it as yours.
  • Glass wall cages hold sleeping birds — break a cage wall to release them (with powers!).
  • Every 5 missile kills earns you a free extra missile.

Map Threats
🐍 SNAKE PACK
A 4-segment chain that relentlessly chases you. A missile hit recoils the snake for 0.5s, removes one head segment, and shortens the chain. A bomb blast also removes one segment. The snake regrows over time. If it gets trapped in a U-shaped wall it will retrace its own path and find another route.
🤖 TBOT (POLLEN BOT)
Your robot helper follows inside your radar circle and collects pollen for you — those pickups count as your score. Its battery dies in 45 seconds. Touch it to revive. Appears as a blue dot on your radar. If it gets trapped in a wall it will retrace and escape.
🦜 WALL SENTRIES
Stationary turrets that perch on walls in groups of 3. They can't move, but they spit pink acid at you within 2 bee-widths and kill on touch. A warning ring pulses when you're in spit range. Use missiles to clear them — they're worth the ammo.
🔒 BIRD CAGES
Glass or circle enclosures containing 2–4 dormant birds. The birds sleep and don't move until a cage wall is broken. Once any wall breaks, they wake up with a random mutant power and scatter outward. Each cage has a wall bomb mounted just outside — plus a 2-bird snake patrol guarding the approach. You'll need to deal with the guards to safely reach the bomb.
Walls
GLASS WALL (cyan glow)
Breakable in one bee touch (without shield). Birds, snake segments, and pollen all bounce off. You can strategically break glass to open paths for birds — or leave them intact to funnel enemies. Glass walls never harden. With shield active, you bounce off glass without breaking it.
SOLID WALL (amber glow)
Unbreakable. No entity — bee, Tbot, bird, or snake — can pass through. Even with shield active, the bee bounces off solid walls. Use them to plan safe corridors and force enemies into predictable paths.
Explosives
  • 🧨 Dynamite — arm by touching, then detonate by touching again. 150px blast radius. Knocks one snake segment off. +55 pts if you trigger it.
  • 💣 Wall Bomb — mounted on a wall. Triggered by bee, missile, or bird contact. 1.5s fuse. Breaks the wall it sits on, chain-blasts nearby bombs. Each cage has one outside it (guarded by a snake patrol).
  • ⚫ Big Bomb — rare Tbot reward shown next to the battery badge. Press SHIFT+B or N to plant. 4-second fuse, 300px blast radius. Get clear before it blows.
Mutant Birds — every 5th bird spawned is a mutant

All birds are white with a teal cap and orange beak. Mutants have a coloured eye and purple crest. Missile kill = +25 pts.

🔥 FIRE
Breathes a fire cone forward every 3.5s. Stay back or move sideways.
💜 LASER
Fires a directional beam forward every 6s. Dodge sideways.
↩⚡ BACK-LASER
Fires a beam behind its flight direction. 2s on / 2s off. Watch your tail.
☠️ POISON
Sprays a toxic cloud every 5s. Contact with the cloud kills.
💪 HEAVY
Larger body (r=22 vs 15), slower. Hard to dodge — takes up more space.
⚡ SHOCK
Electric discharge every 2.5s within 180px radius. Instant kill.
☢️ RADIATION
Emits expanding arc waves every 3s. Rotates 45° each burst. 90° kill cone.
☣ AVIAN FLU
Zebra stripes + yellow bio-eye. Infects pollen it touches. Eating infected pollen starts a 10s death timer. Find the vaccine!
🔀 SPLITTER
Missile kill splits it into 2 smaller birds at ±50°. Kill early before it multiplies!
👻 STEALTH
Fades to 18% opacity after 3s. Reappears within 120px. Shows as a ghost blip on your radar.
💣 BOMBER
Grey eye + pulsing red dot. Drops mines every 6s. Mines explode after 8s.
⛏ DIGGER
Circles a spot and digs a lethal pit (18px wide). Pit stays dangerous for 18s. Avoid the circling arc.
😡 BERSERKER
Red eye grows brighter as it eats. Gains +12% speed per pollen eaten — no cap. Kill it early!
🧲 MAGNET
Blue eye and ring. Pulls all pollen within 200px toward itself. Starves you of your food source.
💨 SMOKE
Grey body. Emits smoke puffs every 0.55s when the bee is nearby, briefly obscuring vision.
❓ ERRATIC
Randomly jinks ±60° every 1.5s and on every wall bounce. Completely unpredictable.
Special Pollen — spawn randomly, each lasts 6 seconds
🌈 RAINBOW
All birds flee instantly. +10 pts on collect.
⚡ SPEED
+20% acceleration. Pink glow aura. +20 pts.
🔮 SPREAD
Triples your pollen collection radius. No bird protection.
🛡 SHIELD
Blocks bird contacts and infection. You still bounce off both glass and solid walls. Deflects ☣ pollen.
❄️ FREEZE
Slows all birds to 50% speed for 6s. Use to escape or set up combos.
🐙 OCTOPUS
Tentacles kill birds (+10–20 pts) and clear ☣ pollen. 2.5× pollen reach.
☣ INFECTED
Eating this starts a 10s death timer. Missile it for +5 pts. It spreads to nearby pollen.
💊 VACCINE
Cures infection immediately. +50 pts. Spawns automatically when you become infected.
